What’s Special About This Drink?

Ragi Buttermilk brings together two powerful elements of traditional Indian nutrition — calcium-rich ragi and cooling, digestive-friendly buttermilk. This unique combination makes it both strengthening and refreshing at the same time.

Ragi has been valued for generations as a wholesome grain that supports stamina and nourishment, while buttermilk keeps the drink light and easy to digest. Shake & Gulp Ragi Buttermilk is designed for modern lifestyles that need natural energy without heaviness.

Because real ragi and curd naturally settle, every bottle is meant to be shaken well before drinking.

Health Benefits

  • Provides natural, sustained energy
  • Supports bone strength with calcium-rich ragi
  • Keeps the body cool and balanced
  • Light on the stomach despite being filling
  • Supports overall nourishment

Who Should Drink This?

Ragi Buttermilk is a wholesome drink for everyone as part of a balanced diet

Ragi is a grain deeply rooted in Indian food traditions — once a staple of everyday meals that kept generations strong. Ragi Buttermilk carries that legacy forward in a simple, drinkable form.

With Shake & Gulp, this age-old nourishment fits into your modern routine — a reminder that true strength often comes from the simplest ingredients.
